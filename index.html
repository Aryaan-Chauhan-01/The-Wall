The Macro View: Expanding the Fiscal Footprint

This section illustrates the overall expansion of the Union Budget. Over the past ten years, total expenditure has nearly tripled. Notice the distinct acceleration post-2020 as the government utilized counter-cyclical fiscal policy—spending heavily during and after the pandemic to stimulate economic recovery. The bar chart visualizes absolute expenditure (in ₹ Lakh Crores), while the line chart overlay indicates the year-on-year growth trajectory.

Sectoral Shift: Reprioritizing the Nation's Wealth

How is the expanded budget being distributed? This visualization focuses on five critical pillars: Defense, Infrastructure (Roads & Railways), Agriculture, Health, and Education. By stacking the allocations, you can observe not just the growth of individual sectors, but their share relative to each other. The most striking trend is the explosive growth of the Infrastructure block, reflecting a deliberate policy shift towards capital expenditure (CapEx) to build long-term economic assets.
